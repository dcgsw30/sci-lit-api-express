About The SciLit Application

We know, citing papers can be a pain! With so many different citation styles (and many professors that are ready to dock off citation marks), it's very important to cite your papers properly. Sci-lit is the perfect application for your homework, research, or general reading needs! Just enter details such as the book title, date, and doi, and the application will store your reading information list on a server- available for you to use anytime! The application also has extra features, such as colour coding, so you know your reading progress for each paper. Whenever you're done creating your reading list, choose your citation style and generate your citation. At this moment, the application only has APA and MLA, which are common citation styles used in research, but more styles are coming. Happy researching!
